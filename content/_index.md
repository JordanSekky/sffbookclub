# About Our Book Club

Welcome to the Pittsburgh Sci-fi & Fantasy Book Club! We're a community of readers passionate about science fiction and fantasy literature. Each month, we gather to discuss a selected book, share our thoughts, and explore the worlds and ideas within these fantastic genres.

Whether you're a longtime fan of speculative fiction or just getting started, we'd love to have you join us!

---

Please email us at [info@sffbook.club](mailto:info@sffbook.club) if you have questions about joining our book club or would like to suggest a book for future meetings.
